Who We Are

"Young Women Campaign Against AIDS (YWCAA) has evolved with time and is progressively moving to promotion of rights and social justice. It was initiated as a merry-go-round (savings and credit scheme) group of young women graduates in 2000. It was registered as a community based organization in 2002, and later as a non govermental organization in 2004 under the NGO Act to address the devastation caused by HIV/AIDS. Life experience at the university in addition to great loss of family members as a result of HIV was a turning point that prompted members to focus on addressing the scourge among vulnerable populations and households in urban informal settlements, and rural poor areas in Kenya. "
